    • If you're a visual artist, don't try to be a writer!! Keep your work separate unless you're smart enough to write into it, simple as that. Have some boundaries. Have a sense of mystery.     • The rest of the museum I was crying too, anytime there was a moment of silence or awe, sense of the expanse of time, moment of connection across and plugged into. Wooden carvings of saints scattered about a spacious room -- tears. Recreated tiled room, elegant patterns, muted palette -- tears. Or at the Episcopal church -- tears, kneeling, looking at Christ, there's a candle in front of him so the heat makes it look like he's moving, shimmering. Crying and praying for something, for suffering & for peace.
    • Death to explanation. Long live the painful mystery.
